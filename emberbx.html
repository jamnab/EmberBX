What is Ember?

An ember sits at the heart of a fire, created by a network of logs and kindling and set ablaze by dedication and focus. As it ignites it launches into the night sky finding its own way on the wind. While still in the fire it helps to ignite other embers, adding to the blaze.

Ember provides women entrepreneurs with opportunities for engagement with a community of colleagues, collaborators, mentors, prospects and funding channels. In doing so it acts as a catalyst for their businesses, bringing together key elements for success.
